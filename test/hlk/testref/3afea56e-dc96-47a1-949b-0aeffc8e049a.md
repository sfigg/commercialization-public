# <span id="p_hlk_test.3afea56e-dc96-47a1-949b-0aeffc8e049a"></span>Camera Driver System Test - MediaCapture - ValidateWhiteBalanceControlValues


This test validates that all White Balance values exposed or permitted by the camera work properly. The test will attempt to take a photo after setting the White balance value.
