# <span id="p_hlk_test.ae30b11c-2e8c-4de4-9dba-708948f53d71"></span>WinUSB 3 Bulk and Interrupt IO Cancellations


This test performs medium sized bulk, interrupt and stream transfers, cancelling after a short timeout.
