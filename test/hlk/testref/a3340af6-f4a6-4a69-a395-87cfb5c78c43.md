# <span id="p_hlk_test.a3340af6-f4a6-4a69-a395-87cfb5c78c43"></span>Camera Sensor Quality Test - Evaluate video frame rate at 640x360 and 80 lux


This Test evaluates the camera sensor's quality by measuring the advertised vs effective frame rate at 640x360 and 80 lux.
