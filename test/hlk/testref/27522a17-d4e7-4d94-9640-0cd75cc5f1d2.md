# <span id="p_hlk_test.27522a17-d4e7-4d94-9640-0cd75cc5f1d2"></span>Camera Driver DirectShow Video Preview - Preview Video Capture Device


This test validates that the camera is able to capture and preview to a render window in DirectShow applications.
